Guacamole

Description

Guacamole is an avocado-based dip or spread that originated in Mexico. It's typically made with mashed avocado and lime juice, then seasoned with salt and cilantro. Guacamole often contains tomatoes and onions.

This easy guacamole recipe is quick and simple to make. Great with tortilla chips or as a topping for Mexican foods!

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados, peeled and pitted
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 ripe tomato, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Mash avocado in a medium serving bowl.
  3. Stir in onion, tomato, and garlic.
  4. Season with lime juice, salt, and pepper.
  5. Cover and chill guacamole for 30 minutes to allow flavors to blend.
